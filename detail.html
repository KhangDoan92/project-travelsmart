Vinh Ha Long

Relinking Beach in
Vinh Ha Long, Quang Ninh

Itinerary

  • Morning: Depart from Hanoi (usually around 8 AM).
    Noon: Arrive in Ha Long, check into the hotel.
    Afternoon: Explore Ha Long Bay by boat. Visit Thien Cung Cave and Ti Top Island.
    Night: Have dinner at a local restaurant and enjoy seafood.

  • Morning: Start the journey on a cruise ship. Visit famous islands like Con Coc Island and Dinh Huong Island.
    Noon: Enjoy lunch on board.
    Afternoon: Go kayaking or explore smaller caves.
    Night: Enjoy dinner and entertainment on the cruise.

  • Morning: Leave the cruise and travel to Cat Ba (by boat or bus).
    Noon: Check into the hotel in Cat Ba.
    Afternoon: Visit Cat Ba National Park, go trekking or cycling.
    Night: Enjoy seafood at local eateries.

  • Morning: Explore Lan Ha Bay by boat. Visit pristine beaches.
    Noon: Rest and have lunch at the beach.
    Afternoon: Participate in activities like snorkeling or kayaking.
    Night: Return to Cat Ba, relax at the hotel.

  • Morning: Free time to stroll around or visit nearby areas.
    Noon: Check out, have lunch, and depart for Hanoi.
    Afternoon: Arrive in Hanoi, end of the trip.
